The BMW M5 F10 was a high-performance sedan produced from 2011 to 2016 featuring a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produced 560 hp (575 hp in Competition models) and 502 lb-ft of torque

It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in about 4.4 seconds, with a top speed electronically limited to 250 km/h (305 km/h with the M Driver's Package).
Key features include a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission (M-DCT), rear-wheel drive, and a torque-vectoring limited-slip differential.
